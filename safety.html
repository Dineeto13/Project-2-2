Do: Store Chemicals away from food storage & contact areas.
Don't: Use a food container (like a cambro) to store chemicals.

Do: Only use a chemical for its intended purpose, & label them clearly in their containers.
Don't: Mix chemicals unless you have been trained.

Do: Clean a surface BEFORE sanitizing it.
Don't: Use a Quat sanitizer under 200 PPM. Check concentration level regularly!
